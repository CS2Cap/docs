CS2Cap is a REST API that aggregates and normalizes market data from 39 CS2 marketplaces — including BUFF163, CSFloat, Skinport, Steam, and more. Whether you're building a trading bot, pricing dashboard, arbitrage scanner, or portfolio tracker, CS2Cap eliminates the work of integrating each marketplace individually and delivers consistent, structured data through a single base URL: `https://api.cs2c.app/v1`.

## Key capabilities

| Capability | Endpoints | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Price data** | `/v1/prices`, `/v1/prices/history` | Current lowest asks and historical candles across providers |
| **Buy orders** | `/v1/bids` | Highest buy order snapshots from bid-enabled marketplaces |
| **Sales** | `/v1/sales` | Completed transaction records with price and timestamp |
| **Candle data** | `/v1/prices/candles` | OHLCV candles aggregated across providers |
| **Analytics** | `/v1/market/indicators`, `/v1/market/arbitrage` | Technical indicators, arbitrage edges, and liquidity scores |
| **Portfolio** | `/v1/portfolio` | Track holdings, import from Steam, and value in real time |
| **FX conversion** | `/v1/fx`, `?currency=` param | On-the-fly currency conversion on all price fields |
| **Item catalog** | `/v1/items`, `/v1/providers` | Item identity, metadata, phase, wear, and provider details |

## Tiers

CS2Cap offers three tiers. All tiers use the same endpoints and authentication model — tier determines your rate limits, monthly quota, and which endpoints are accessible.

| Tier | Requests/min | Requests/month | Max `limit` | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | 20 | 1,000 | 100 | Prices endpoint only |
| **Pro** | 100 | 500,000 | 1,000 | Prices, bids, sales, portfolio |
| **Quant** | 300 | 1,000,000 | 1,000 | All endpoints including analytics |

<Note>
  Buy orders, sales history, and market analytics endpoints require a Pro or Quant key. Free keys are limited to the `/v1/prices` endpoint.
</Note>
